Black-Scholes equation:
\begin{equation}
    \frac{\partial V}{\partial t}
    +
    \frac{1}{2} \sigma^2 S^2 
    \frac{\partial^2V}{\partial S^2}
    =
    rV
    -
    rS\frac{\partial V}{\partial S}
\end{equation}
